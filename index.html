Rock Paper Scissors Game Director's Cut

Welcome to the rock, paper, scissors game! I'll explain a few things if you're new.

The game will last until either you, or the computer reaches 5 points.

Each player picks either rock, paper, or scissors. Rock beats scissors, paper beats rock, scissors beat paper.

Each round gives the winner 1 point unless it's a draw. Then no one gets a point.
